How to connect your Google Sheet

  1. Open your Google Sheet and go to File → Share → Publish to web
  2. In the dropdowns, select your sheet tab and Comma-separated values (.csv)
  3. Click Publish and copy the link
  4. Paste the link into the Google Sheet CSV URL box in the header above and click Load

Your sheet must have these columns: Start_time, location_name, title, description, and optionally the host name columns. Every time someone opens this page it will pull the latest data — just keep your sheet updated!
